--- highlight: true authors: - Shubiao Pan - Zhenyuan Lu - Risheng jin - Ying Wang - Jun Lv patent: https://patents.google.com/patent/CN104069023B/en pdf: https://patentimages.storage.googleapis.com/55/5d/0c/2ca988eba57d8e/CN104069023B.pdf title: A kind of thickener blend composition and the application in cosmetics thereof year: 2014 --- The invention discloses a kind of thickener blend composition and the application in cosmetics thereof.This thickener blend composition comprises Scleroglucan and double decyl tetradecyl alcohol polyethers 20 ether of PEG 240/HDI copolymer as thickening agent.In a preferred embodiment, in compositions, the weight ratio of Scleroglucan and double decyl tetradecyl alcohol polyethers 20 ether of PEG 240/HDI copolymer is 1:20 to 20:1.The invention also discloses said composition application in the cosmetics comprising electrolyte.
