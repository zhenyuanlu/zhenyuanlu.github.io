--- highlight: true authors: - Shubiao Pan - Zhenyuan Lu - Risheng jin - Jun Lv patent: https://patents.google.com/patent/CN104069024B/en pdf: https://patentimages.storage.googleapis.com/62/33/2c/f30532002200d2/CN104069024B.pdf title: A thickening agent composition and applications thereof in cosmetics year: 2014 --- The invention discloses a kind of thickener blend composition and its application in cosmetics.The thickener blend composition is comprising Scleroglucan and ammonium acryloyldime-thyltaurate/25 Methacrylate Crosspolymer of docosyl alcohol polyethers as thickener.In a preferred embodiment, in composition, the weight ratio of Scleroglucan and ammonium acryloyldime-thyltaurate/25 Methacrylate Crosspolymer of docosyl alcohol polyethers is 1:20 to 20:1.The invention also discloses application of the said composition in the cosmetics comprising electrolyte.
